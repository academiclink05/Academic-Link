Rooms & Facilities

Academic Link Girls' PG offers neat, air-conditioned rooms with attached washrooms and all basic furniture. Rooms are ideal for 2–3 girls and are maintained daily for cleanliness and comfort.

Room Setup

Each room is designed to give every girl her own space and comfort while sharing the room with others. Every girl gets a single bed and basic furniture so that daily living and study time stay organised.

Per girl, the room includes:

  • Single bed with mattress.
  • Wardrobe / storage space.
  • Study table and chair.

Room features:

  • 2–3 girls per room.
  • Fan and AC in room.
  • Attached washroom with the room.

Facilities & Safety

The PG is managed with personal attention to safety, discipline and cleanliness. It is suitable for girls and parents who want a secure and homely environment in Jaipur.

  • Managed by a mother and daughter.
  • Girls-only accommodation – no boys allowed inside rooms.
  • Society guard for entry and basic security.
  • 24×7 CCTV surveillance in common areas.
  • Daily room cleaning to maintain hygiene.
  • Wi‑Fi available for students and working girls.
