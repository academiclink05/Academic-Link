About Academic Link

Academic Link is located in Raja Park, Jaipur, a central and lively area known for its markets, cafes, coaching institutes, and family-friendly surroundings. We offer tuition classes from 1st to 10th for CBSE, RBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, IB, and Cambridge boards, along with Spoken English classes.

Why Academic Link?

Every child deserves personal attention and a calm learning space. At Academic Link, we focus on understanding each student’s needs, building confidence, and strengthening core concepts.

  • ✔ Small batches for better focus
  • ✔ Personal guidance for every child
  • ✔ Friendly and stress-free learning

Girls' PG in Raja Park, Jaipur

Academic Link also offers safe and comfortable girls' PG accommodation in Raja Park, Jaipur. The PG includes AC rooms, attached bathrooms, spacious rooms, and food facility on request.

It is a girls-only stay managed by a mother and daughter team, designed to provide a homely and secure atmosphere.

About Us

Academic Link began as an educational space focused on learning and guidance. Today, it has grown into a safe, comfortable, and study-friendly girls' PG in Raja Park, Jaipur.

Our aim is to provide girls with a clean, peaceful, and homely place to stay. The PG is thoughtfully arranged for daily comfort, with proper room space, storage, and a calm environment suitable for students and working girls.

What Makes Us Special

  • Safe girls-only accommodation
  • Neat and comfortable room setup
  • Storage and essential furniture available
  • Peaceful and study-friendly environment
  • Convenient location in Raja Park, Jaipur
  • Family-managed and homely atmosphere

We focus on making the stay simple, secure, and comfortable so that girls feel at ease like home.
