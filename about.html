About Academic Link Girls' PG

Academic Link Girls' PG is a safe and calm accommodation option for girls in Raja Park, Jaipur. It is designed for students and working women who want a neat, homely and study-friendly place to stay.

Our Story

Academic Link started as a small, student-focused initiative to support learning and personal growth. Over time, we realised that many girls coming to Jaipur for studies also needed a safe and comfortable place to live.

Academic Link Girls' PG was created to meet this need – a girls-only stay that feels homely, peaceful and organised. The focus is on cleanliness, safety, and a calm environment where girls can study and relax without disturbance.

Who is this PG for?

  • College students studying in and around Raja Park.
  • Girls attending nearby coaching centres.
  • Working women looking for a neat, quiet place to stay.

Location Advantage – Raja Park, Jaipur

Raja Park is one of the most convenient areas in Jaipur, with easy access to markets, coaching institutes, cafes and public transport. Staying here saves travel time and helps girls manage their daily routine comfortably.

  • Close to coaching institutes and main roads.
  • Daily needs and food options available nearby.
  • Easy connectivity to other parts of Jaipur.

Our Approach

We believe that a good PG is more than just a room – it is a safe space where girls feel cared for and respected. At Academic Link Girls' PG, we maintain a peaceful environment, clear rules, and regular cleaning to keep the space comfortable.

  • Focus on safety and discipline.
  • Clean and organised common areas.
  • Supportive and respectful atmosphere for all residents.
