About Academic Link Girls' PG

Academic Link Girls' PG offers a safe, clean and comfortable stay for girls in Raja Park, Jaipur. It is a girls-only PG managed with personal care, discipline and a family-friendly environment.

Our aim is to provide a peaceful place where students and working women can stay with comfort, security and confidence.

Who We Are

Academic Link Girls' PG is a secure accommodation option for students and working women who want a peaceful and well-managed place to stay. The PG is run by a mother and daughter team, which gives residents a more personal, caring and homely environment.

Our focus is on safety, cleanliness and comfort so that every girl can stay with confidence. The location in Raja Park makes daily life convenient, with nearby markets, coaching areas, transport and essential services.

Safe Environment

The PG is girls-only, with no boys allowed inside the room. The property also has society guard support and 24x7 CCTV surveillance for added security.

Family Management

Managed by a mother and daughter, the PG maintains a disciplined and respectful atmosphere. This creates a more comfortable and trustworthy living experience for residents and parents.

Clean & Comfortable Stay

Rooms are maintained with daily cleaning and essential furniture for easy living. Wi-Fi is available, and the setup is suitable for study, rest and daily routine.

Ideal For

This PG is suitable for college students, coaching students and working women looking for a calm and secure stay in Jaipur. It is especially useful for girls who want a neat, family-managed place in Raja Park.
