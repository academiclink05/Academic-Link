About Academic Link Institute

Academic Link Institute is a friendly neighbourhood coaching centre in Raja Park, Jaipur, focused on strong concepts and confidence for school students up to Class 10.

The institute offers tuition for all subjects and boards, with extra care for weak students and those shifting from Hindi to English medium.

About Pujita Ahuja

MA, B.Ed with more than seven years of teaching experience in school and coaching environments.

Believes in breaking every topic into simple steps, using real‑life examples and continuous revision so children remember what they learn.

Teaching Approach

Small batches of around three students, regular tests, homework checking and feedback to parents.

Focus on clarity of basics, written practice and stress‑free learning so students enjoy studying.
